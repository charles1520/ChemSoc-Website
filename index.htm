Welcome to the UW Chemical Engineering Student Society (UW CESS)

We are the University of Waterloo Student Chapter of the Canadian Society of Chemical Engineers (CSChE) as well as the UW Student Chapter of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE).

Our student-run office is located on the main floor of DWE in room 2528. We are open from 11:30am-1:30pm on weekdays so feel free to stop by and see us! We sell the classic, red Enjoy Chem Eng T-shirts, and lab coats silk-screened with the logo pictured in the top left corner! We have a microwave available for students to warm up their lunch.

New Office: We are now located in DWE 2528. Our new office has much more room than the old one (room for around 10 people to work comfortably), and we even have some new furniture!
